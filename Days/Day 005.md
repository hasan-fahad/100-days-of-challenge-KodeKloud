## âš™ï¸ Good to Know: SELinux

### **Purpose**

Mandatory Access Control (MAC) security framework for Linux.

### **Modes**

* **Enforcing**: Policies enforced, violations blocked
* **Permissive**: Policies logged, not enforced
* **Disabled**: SELinux completely off

### **Policies**

* Targeted (default)
* Strict
* MLS (Multi-Level Security)

### **Security Context**

Every file/process has a context: `user:role:type:level`
